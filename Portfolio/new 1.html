Matt's Diner is family owned and community focused for over 15 years.
Starting back in 2005 we've made it our goal to only use organic produce and meat from our local Virginia farms.
We pride ourselves on being a casual, "farm to table" restaurant featuring southern cuisine.
We are open for lunch and dinner with brunch being available on Sundays.
We are also available for carryout as well! We know not everyone has the time to dine in and we don't want you to miss out!
